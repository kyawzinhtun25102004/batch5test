Biography

Htoo Eain Thin was one of the most influential singers and songwriters in Myanmar. He was admired for his emotional voice, meaningful lyrics, and creative musical style.

Throughout his career, he released many successful songs and inspired countless music lovers across the country. His works remain popular among different generations.

Even after his passing in 2004, his music continues to live on and he is remembered as one of Myanmar's greatest artists.
